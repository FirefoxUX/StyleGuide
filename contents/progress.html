Progress Indicators

Progress Bars

Use progress bars when the duration of the task being performed can be predicted, for example, when importing bookmarks.

Activity Bars

Use activity bars when the duration of the task cannot be predicted and may take a long time, for example, when downloading files.

Activity Spinners

Use activity spinners when the duration of the task being performed cannot be predicted, when space is constrained, or when the task is expected to complete quickly, for example, when waiting for a server to respond.
